HAILIE Programme

Events and recordings

HAILIE runs short, high-value virtual sessions and roundtables on responsible AI in social housing every four weeks. Join the mailing list to receive invites.

How HAILIE sessions work

HAILIE runs free, vendor-neutral virtual sessions approximately every four weeks. Sessions are typically 45–60 minutes and include a short talk, peer discussion or themed breakouts.

Some sessions are themed — exploring AI governance, tenant impact, specific tools, case studies or automation. Others are open for sharing current challenges and progress.

Recordings are published publicly on YouTube where available. Summaries are shared with mailing list subscribers.

Join the mailing list for invites
Free and vendor-neutral

All HAILIE sessions are free. There are no sales pitches. Vendors and consultants can participate transparently but not to promote their products.
