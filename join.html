What you get

Practical value from every session

HAILIE is built by and for housing professionals. Everything shared in the community is practical, honest and focused on what works — not what vendors want you to think works.

The exchange runs approximately every four weeks. Signing up is free and commits you to nothing beyond receiving the mailing list.

Invites to roundtables — short, vendor-neutral virtual sessions on housing-specific AI topics, run approximately every four weeks.

Summaries and recordings — access to session notes and video recordings from all past HAILIE sessions.

AI briefings and guidance — practical resources for boards, IT leaders, service managers and small providers.

Reusable templates and checklists — governance frameworks, DPIA templates, vendor evaluation tools and experiment templates you can adapt immediately.

Peer learning — an honest community of housing professionals sharing what works, what does not and what they are learning as they go.

Who should join

HAILIE is for everyone who cares about getting AI right in housing

The three primary routes are for senior leaders, IT and data teams, and service leaders — but the community is open to everyone in or around housing.

Chief executives, board members and executive directors

IT directors, data leads, digital and technology teams

Service managers, COOs, customer experience and transformation leads

Governance, risk, compliance and information governance leads

Tenant voice, scrutiny and resident engagement leads

Small registered providers and local authority housing teams

Frontline managers, AI champions and community of practice leads

Vendors, consultants, researchers and sector bodies — participating transparently without sales pressure

Get involved

How to contribute to HAILIE

HAILIE is a community exchange. The more people contribute, the more useful it becomes for everyone. Here are ways to get involved beyond joining the mailing list:

Suggest a topic

Share an idea for a session theme, question or challenge you would like to explore with peers.

Offer to speak

Share a case study, practical demonstration or lessons-learned session with the community.

Share a template or artefact

Contribute a checklist, policy, experiment template, board paper or practical document for the community to reuse.

Ask a question

Bring a challenge, concern or open question to the community — others are likely facing the same thing.

Run a hackathon or experiment

Volunteer to lead a practical experiment session and share what you learn with the community.

Get in touch

Contact the HAILIE Chair directly at guy@housingai.org with any contribution, question or idea.
